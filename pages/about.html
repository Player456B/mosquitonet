About Raj Marketing

Your trusted partner for premium mosquito nets in Mysore since 2001

Welcome to Raj Marketing

Established in 2016, Raj Marketing has been serving the people of Mysore with premium quality mosquito nets and window solutions for over two decades. What started as a small shop in Shivarampet has now grown into one of Mysore's most trusted names for home protection solutions [citation:2].

Founded by Mr. Govinda Raju, our journey began with a simple mission: to provide every home in Mysore with affordable, durable, and effective mosquito protection. Today, we have successfully installed mosquito nets in over 5,000 homes across Mysore city [citation:2].

Our Location

We are strategically located in the heart of Mysore at #65, Near Pragathi logistics, Hootghalli industries area, Hootghalli, Mysuru -570018 , near the famous K.T. Street. Our central location makes it easy for customers from all parts of the city to visit us for consultations and measurements [citation:2].

Our Commitment

At Raj Marketing, we believe in quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction. Every product we sell comes with a comprehensive warranty, and our installation team is trained to deliver perfection. We use only premium materials sourced from trusted manufacturers to ensure your family's safety [citation:1].
