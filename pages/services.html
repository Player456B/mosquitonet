Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

Q: How long does installation take?

A: For a typical 3 BHK home, installation takes 4-6 hours. We complete the job in a single day with minimal disturbance.

Q: Do you provide free estimates?

A: Yes, we provide free on-site measurement and quotation with no obligation. Call us to schedule a visit.

Q: What is your service area?

A: We serve all areas within Mysore city limits. For locations outside the city, please contact us for availability.

Q: How do I clean the mosquito nets?

A: Simply wipe with a soft damp cloth. For deep cleaning, you can remove the nets (if detachable) and wash with mild soap.
